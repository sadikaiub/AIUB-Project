Our hotel also offers guests a luxury apartment for up to eight people over a floor space of 185 m2.This apartment is situated on the top floor of the hotel.

The Radium rooftop apartment boasts a fitted kitchen, a dining room, a work area with Internet connection and a living room with plasma TV, satellite and DVD player.All these rooms are connected and offer generous space.

You will also find three separate bedrooms, a walk-in wardrobe, storage space for luggage and two bathrooms,one of which is fitted with a washing machine, the other with a bath and shower unit.

The apartment is fully air-conditioned. A safe, an elevator directly to the apartment and the services of reception 24 hours a day are all a matter of course.

Twenty-four hour reception service available and parking possibilities in the hotel garage.
